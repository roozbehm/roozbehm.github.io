Background

I am a Senior AI Research Scientist Manager at FAIR and an Affiliate Associate Professor in Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington.

Prior to joining FAIR, I was the Research Manager of the PRIOR team at the Allen Institute for AI. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Stanford University. I received my Ph.D. from UCLA, advised by Alan Yuille.
